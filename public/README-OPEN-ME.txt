COBA site — how to open (for your client)
==========================================

1. Unzip the archive
2. Open the folder: cobagold-site
3. Windows: double-click START-WINDOWS.bat
   macOS: double-click START-MAC.command

Your browser should open http://127.0.0.1:5173/ or http://localhost:5173/

Do not open index.html by double-clicking — use the START script so the wallet
QR and live prices work. Keep the black window open while viewing the site.
