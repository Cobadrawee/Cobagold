ICC "Universum" is a non-profit organization that brings together groups of interrelated companies, associations, and creative teams of ICC "Universum" shareholders. It was founded by a highly qualified, socially oriented team of specialists to implement a broad set of programs and high-tech projects aimed at improving quality of life, creating a comfortable and environmentally sound living environment, and unlocking people’s creative potential.

Based on ICC "Universum", a significant Unit Fund was formed, including shareholders’ commodity inventories, precious and rare-earth metals, agricultural and industrial land plots, deposits of precious stones and metals, peat, silicon, manganese, and more; debt instruments: NATIONAL BOND OF DAIICHI KANGYO BANK, TOKYO, JAPAN (N157602-861) and NATIONAL BOND OF DAIICHI KANGYO BANK, TOKYO, JAPAN (N157602-887); the certified documentary draft (tratta) UCC-2 Ford Motor Company; receivables from the global financial system (transaction code No. TV-BCT AN 10-01-07-0054-02.17); and sufficient bank gold reserves for 100% backing of all crypto-bills issued and circulated as tokens.

Within the financial and logistics platform, COBA cryptocurrency tokens were introduced, gold-backed (1 token = 9.6 grams of gold), with reserves exceeding issuance volume, together with the unit and charter funds of the cooperative group of companies.
