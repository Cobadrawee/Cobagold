ICC "Universum & Comcon" is a non-profit organization that brings together groups of interrelated companies, associations, and creative teams of shareholders of ICC "Universum & Comcon", in cooperation with the Agency for Public Safety (Switzerland). It was founded by highly qualified, socially oriented specialists with the goal of implementing a wide range of programs and high-tech projects aimed at improving people’s quality of life and creating a comfortable, environmentally clean living environment, as well as unlocking their creative potential.

Based on ICC "Universum & Comcon", a significant Unit Fund was formed, including shareholders’ commodity inventories, precious and rare-earth metals, land plots for agricultural and industrial purposes, deposits of precious stones, metals, peat, silicon, manganese, and more; debt obligations: NATIONAL BOND OF DAIICHI KANGYO BANK, TOKYO, JAPAN (N157602-861) and NATIONAL BOND OF DAIICHI KANGYO BANK, TOKYO, JAPAN (N157602-887); as well as the certified documentary draft (tratta) UCC-2 Ford Motor Company, receivables from the global financial system (transaction code №TV-BCT AN 10-01-07-0054-02.17), and sufficient volumes of bank gold reserves to provide 100% backing for the entire amount of crypto-bills issued and circulated in the form of tokens.

Within the created financial and logistics platform, COBA cryptocurrency’s own tokens were issued and are backed by gold coverage (1 token = 9.6 grams of gold), with reserves exceeding the issuance volume, as well as unit and charter funds of the cooperative group companies.

COBA is designed for comprehensive financing of socially significant projects, deployment of advanced technologies, alternative energy, and large-scale environmental initiatives aimed at cleaning and environmentally correcting water, land, and forest resources, and more. Ensuring maximum safety and comfort for investors is one of the key priorities. COBA is a decentralized digital currency developed to improve the security and convenience of transactions on the blockchain. It uses modern encryption algorithms to protect user data and prevent fraudulent activity.
